    • 2156, June :

      Endeavour responds to a distress signal from SS Nightingale (UES 9016), which has surrendered to a Romulan Bird of Prey. The Nightingale is destroyed by the Romulans, but not before the Endeavour crew discovered a Romulan spy aboard.
